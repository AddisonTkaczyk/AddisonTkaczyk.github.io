Athletes United

Athletes United ("Athletes U") provides dedicated athletes with Elite hockey skills training to help players take their game to the next level. Currently available in Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, and surrounding areas.

Get Started

Welcome to
Athletes United Hockey

Athletes U training methods provide the environment and structure for players to focus on improving their individual skills, confidence, and game with professional guidance from trained and experienced Skills Coaches.
We offer year round training for dedicated athletes looking to reach the next level.

Can I enroll? Athletes U is for anyone trying to improve their game. Our programs help athletes improve individual skills and confidence to help reach their goals.
"You are your own competition. Be better than you were yesterday".

Summer Skill Development Program

We offer a six week skill development program Jul-Aug that you can register in, each session focuses on a different skill. We offer skill sessions for Atom, Peewee, and Bantam.


Skill Sessions

  • Week 1 - Power skating
  • Week 2 - Power skating + puck control
  • Week 3 - Puck control
  • Week 4 - Puck control + Passing and reciveing
  • Week 5 - Power skating + Rushing w/puck
  • Week 6 - Power skating + puck control
