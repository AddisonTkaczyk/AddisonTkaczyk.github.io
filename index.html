Athletes United Hockey

Athletes U training methods provide the environment and structure for a player to work hard on their skills and improve their game and confidence with professional guidance from trained, experienced and seasoned Skills Coaches.
Athletes U offers year round training for dedicated athletes looking to reach the next level.

Can I enroll? Athletes U is for anyone trying to improve their game - what ever the skill set we will help you reach your goals. Most of our programs help students improve individual skills and confidence in a group setting.
You are your own competition to be better than you were yesterday.

Summer Skill Development Program

We offer a 6 week skill development program July- Aug that you can register in, each session focuses on a different skill.
We offer skill sessions for Atom, Pewwe, and Bantam. You can attend register for sessions individually or register for all sessions for a discounted rate (Recommended).


Skill Sessions

  • week 1 - Power skating
  • week 2 - Power skating + puck control
  • week 3 - Puck control
  • week 4 - puck control + Passing and reciveing
  • week 5 - power skating + Rushing w/puck
  • week 6 - Power skating + puck control
