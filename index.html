Athletes U Hockey

Providing Elite hockey skills training to Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, and surrounding areas to dedicated athletes who want to take their game to the next level.

Get Started

Welcome to
Athletes United Hockey

Athletes U training methods provide the environment and structure for a player to work hard on their skills and improve their game and confidence with professional guidance from trained, experienced and seasoned Skills Coaches.
We offer year round training for dedicated athletes looking to reach the next level.

Can I enroll? Athletes U is for anyone trying to improve their game. Our programs help athletes improve individual skills and confidence to help reach their goals.
You are your own competition to be better than you were yesterday.

Our Programs

Athletes U offers Skill Development programs as well as camps year round.

Summer Skill Development Program

We offer a 6 Week skill development program July- Aug that you can register in, each session focuses on a different skill.
We offer skill sessions for Atom, Peewee, and Bantam. You can attend register for sessions individually or register for all sessions for a discounted rate (Recommended).


Skill Sessions

  • Week 1 - Power skating
  • Week 2 - Power skating + puck control
  • Week 3 - Puck control
  • Week 4 - Puck control + Passing and reciveing
  • Week 5 - Power skating + Rushing w/puck
